Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Row Swift is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

1. No Data Collection

Row Swift does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or CSV file contents. All processing is done entirely on your device.

2. On-Device Processing

Imported CSV files, descriptive statistics, and visualizations are generated locally and never leave your device. The app does not connect to external servers or services for data processing.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

Row Swift does not use analytics tools, tracking frameworks, or third-party advertising networks. The app does not gather any information about how you use it.

4. Third-Party Services

Row Swift does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services. If you export data or images using the system share sheet, the handling of those exports is governed by your device and chosen apps.

5. Changes to This Policy

The developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the effective date above.
